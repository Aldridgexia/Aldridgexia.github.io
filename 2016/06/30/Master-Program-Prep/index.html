Math

Linear Algebra

  • Textbook:
    • Linear Algebra and Its Applications 4ed.MIT Gilbert Strang
  • Topics:
    • vector spaces, vectors, matrices, types of matrices, matrix transposes, determinants, inverses, linear transformation, eigenvalues and eigenvectors, spectral decomposition, linear dependence, rank, positive and negative definite matrices, vector spaces, partitioned matrices.

Calculus

  • Textbook:
    • 简明微积分教程
  • Topics:
    • Functions, continuity, convexity, concavity, derivatives, gradients, Jacobians, Hessians, implicit function theorem, constrained optimization, Lagrange multipliers, Kuhn-Tucker conditions, maximum principle, envelope theorem.

Probability

  • Textbook:
    • 陈希孺文集·概率论与数理统计
  • Topics:
    • Axiomatic foundations of probability, state space, probability measures, continuous and discrete random variables, distributions and densities of random variables, important parametric families of distributions.
    • Expectations, variance, covariance, moments (mean, variance, skewness, kurtosis), quantiles, independence, conditioning, conditional expectations, conditional distributions, correlation and independence.
    • Characteristic functions, modes of convergence, laws of large numbers, central limit theorems, Bayes theorem.

Stochastic Processes

  • Textbook:
    • Shreve I, II, Stochastic Processes by Ross
  • Topics:
    • Filtration, martingales, linear processes, random walks, autoregressive and moving average processes, stationarity, processes with independent increments, martingales, submartingales, supermartingales, Brownian motion.
